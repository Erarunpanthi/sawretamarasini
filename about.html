About Me

I am Aarav Sharma, a dedicated Law student currently in my 5th semester at the Faculty of Law, Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu. My academic journey has been shaped by a deep commitment to justice, equity, and the rule of law.

I have engaged in legal research, moot court competitions, community legal awareness programs, and internships with renowned law firms. I am passionate about constitutional law, human rights, and access to justice for marginalised communities.

My long-term aspiration is to contribute meaningfully to the development of Nepal's legal system — whether through advocacy, academia, public interest litigation, or policy reform. I believe that legal professionals have a fundamental duty to serve not just their clients, but society as a whole.

"The law is reason, free from passion." — Aristotle
