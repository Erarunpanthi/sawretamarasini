Areas of Legal Interest

The legal domains I study, research, and am passionate about.

Constitutional Law

Studying the foundational laws of Nepal's 2015 Constitution, fundamental rights, directive principles, and constitutional bodies with a focus on judicial review.

Fundamental Rights Judicial Review Directive Principles
Key Subjects Covered:
Constitution of Nepal 2015, Writ Jurisdiction, Separation of Powers, Fundamental Rights (Arts. 16–46), Constitutional Commission Law

Human Rights Law

Researching international and domestic human rights frameworks, the role of NHRC, and protection of marginalised communities under Nepali law.

NHRC International Law Marginalised Communities
Key Subjects Covered:
ICCPR, ICESCR, CEDAW, CRC, NHRC Act 2012, Paris Principles, International Humanitarian Law

Criminal Law & Procedure

In-depth study of the National Penal Code 2017, criminal procedure, evidence law, and victim rights within Nepal's justice system.

Penal Code 2017 Victim Rights Evidence Law
Key Subjects Covered:
National Penal Code 2074, Criminal Procedure Code, Evidence Act, Domestic Violence Act, Juvenile Justice

Civil & Family Law

Exploring civil procedure, property rights, contract law, and family law regulations including marriage, divorce, and inheritance under Nepali statute.

Contract Law Property Rights Family Law
Key Subjects Covered:
Civil Procedure Code, Contract Act, Muluki Civil Code 2074, Land Acquisition Act, Inheritance Laws

Environmental Law

Studying environmental protection laws, climate justice, and the intersection of constitutional rights with environmental governance in Nepal.

Climate Justice Right to Clean Environment
Key Subjects Covered:
National Parks Act, Environment Protection Act 2076, Paris Agreement, Right to Clean Environment (Art. 30)

Labour & Employment Law

Research into the Labour Act 2017, workers' rights, trade union law, and dispute resolution mechanisms in Nepali workplaces.

Labour Act 2017 Workers' Rights Trade Unions
Key Subjects Covered:
Labour Act 2074, Social Security Act, Trade Union Act, Foreign Employment Act, ILO Conventions
